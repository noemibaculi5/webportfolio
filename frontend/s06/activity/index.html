Kamado Tanjiro

Who is Kamado Tanjiro ?


Kamado Tanjiro (竈門炭治郎) is the main protagonist of the popular Japanese manga and anime series Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba, created by Koyoharu Gotouge. Tanjiro starts as a kindhearted boy who lives with his family in the mountains, making a living by selling charcoal. One day, he returns home to find his family slaughtered by demons, with only Nezuko surviving — but transformed into a demon herself. Determined to avenge his family and cure Nezuko, Tanjiro joins the Demon Slayer Corps, a secret organization dedicated to hunting demons and protecting humanity.
